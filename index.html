Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder, All-Purpose Flour, Baking Powder, Ghee, Milk
  • Ghee or Oil (for frying)
  • Sugar, Water, Cardamom Pods, Saffron (optional), Rose Water (optional), Lemon Juice

Recipe

  • Prepare the sugar syrup.
  • Make the dough.
  • Fry the balls.
  • Soak in syrup, garnish it with some Dryfriuts and enjoy

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian Cottage Cheese), All-Purpose Flour
  • Milk, Sugar, Cardamom Pods, Saffron, Pistachios, Almonds
  • Lemon Juice or Vinegar (for curdling milk)

Recipe

  • Make paneer, shape into balls.
  • Prepare sugar syrup, cook paneer balls in syrup.
  • Make thickened milk (rabri) with sugar, cardamom, and saffron.
  • Soak paneer balls in rabri, garnish with nuts.

Kheer

Ingredients

  • Rice, Milk, Sugar
  • Cardamom Pods, Saffron (optional)
  • Almonds, Pistachios, Raisins

Recipe

  • Cook rice in milk until tender.
  • Add sugar, cardamom, saffron, and simmer.
  • Garnish with almonds, pistachios, and raisins.
  • Serve chilled or warm.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Ingredients

  • Grated Carrots, Milk, Sugar
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter), Cardamom Powder
  • Almonds, Cashews, Raisins

Recipe

  • Cook grated carrots in ghee.
  • Add milk, cook until milk reduces.
  • Add sugar and cardamom powder, cook until thick.
  • Garnish with almonds, cashews, and raisins.
